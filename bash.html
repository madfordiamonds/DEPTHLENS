What makes this redesign different
Every decision is intentional — not just a visual upgrade, but a clarity upgrade.
🎯

Serif + Mono pairing

DM Serif Display for headlines gives warmth and intelligence. DM Mono for labels and data gives precision. Instrument Sans for body — readable at small sizes.

🌊

Layer-color language

Each of the 10 reality layers gets its own persistent accent color. Users instantly recognize Layer 9 (Root Cause) by its rose tone across all screens.

✦

Micro-surface depth

4 surface levels (surface-1 through surface-4) create visual hierarchy. Cards float above backgrounds. Active states use border glow, not background fill.

◎

Analysis cards as first-class UI

The diagnostic result card is designed like a premium data instrument — layer activation bars, probability tracks, strength pips — all at a glance.

⚡

Mode chips in input bar

Analysis mode is always visible and switchable inline — no modal needed. The active chip glows in violet so users always know their current lens.

🎨

4 shipping themes

Void (deep dark), Deep Sea (blue-dark), Polar Dawn (light), Ember (warm dark). All designed from the same token system — swap them instantly.
